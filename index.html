Patient Management Information System

After analyzing the current process, the proposed system was broken into several sections to approach the achievable standards with the consideration of functional requirements. Patient Management is one of the most important modules which most of the data will be handled mainly by the Medical Staff. Reports regarding the patients & search options are also available along with Medical Staff Management etc...


Paying Ward Management Information System

When working in an intense environment, accuracy of data (by implementing data validation techniques) is vital for treatments & payments, while on the other hand user friendliness is a key issue to work with the system. Therefore dropdown menus, images, icons, message prompts, familiar interfaces & many other mechanisms were used.Highly concerned with the security in accessing patients medical records, password protection algorithms etc.


Communication, notification & a high alert System

Internal system users can be informed by creating messages or emails using this sysytem & a user generated notification should appear on the header with the count, on the drop down menu like fb style. If the user on that page click & enter then it should disappear. Alerts should be seen on the top right corner of the notification page.


User Management System

Managing the users according to their ID is vital as they have been given different access levels to add, edit, delete & view. The development officer as the administrator was assigned with the responsibility of managing this system.


Clinic Management System

Here a patient can be registered only once & can be sent for six different clinics & then the next clinic date will be automatically updated along with the patient's age calculation & prescription as well as giving a high privacy for medical records & reports etc...


Reports Handling System

This should facilitate for various kinds of report genaration for Profits, Expenses, PCU, IC as well as for the Accounts Receivable, & other management areas that will be used to generate charts/graphs. Different sorting methods were used to categorize the relevant information for instance in terms of the date, reports can be generated daily, monthly, annually etc..


Drug store & Pharmacy Management System

Maintaining the log book for medicine with some amount of calculations, which are automatically done & various reports/charts/graphs can be genarated according to many services assisted for various sections.


Inventory Management Information System

In order to maximize the productivity which goes along with maintanence & profits; this MIS was implemented. When handling sensitive data; image processing system was also used .


Schedule Management Information System

One key part to the success of an organization is to consider the effiency & the effectiveness of the work force with a fine awareness along with a smooth management.Therefor this automated system was built to maximize day today productivity, developed with various modern options.


Ward Management Information System

Considered as the center of a hospital management which combines many sections such as Internal Care, Pilminary Cate Unit etc.So this automated system facilitate functional requirements like handling beding records including medical updates as well as day today critical management activities. On the other hand non-functional requirements, for instance expandability, interoperarbility were implemented as well.


UI/UX Designs for Hospital Management System

Concerning mental & physical health awareness & well-being of the users; calm & attractive color combinations for different user interfaces were used, covering the entire hospital management service up to the fullest extent as possible!


Graphs/Charts Handling System

This can be used to compare & analyze the current overall process which will facilitate for various management areas. Different sorting methods were used to categorize the relevant information connecting to the data bases & also can be devloped with AI techniques as well!
